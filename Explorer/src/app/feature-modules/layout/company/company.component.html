Who we are
Kurbanov Solutions is the visionary team behind the creation of Explorer. As a dynamic and innovative company, we take pride in our roots in Novi Sad, Serbia. Comprising 16 dedicated employees, each member is a computer science student at the Faculty of Technical Sciences, bringing a wealth of technical expertise and a passion for cutting-edge solutions to the table. Our journey began with a shared commitment to redefining the travel experience through technology, and Explorer is a testament to the collaborative spirit and dedication of our team. With a collective focus on crafting intuitive and immersive solutions, Kurbanov Solutions is driven by a desire to make exploration more accessible and enjoyable for travelers worldwide. Join us on this exciting journey, as we continue to push the boundaries of innovation and redefine the future of travel experiences.
